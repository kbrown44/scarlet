Scarlet Studios is a digital imaging company owned and operated by Kyle Brown. We provide photographic services such as headshots, wedding and event photography, portraits and digital retouching. We also provide video services including video editing, VFX, motion graphics, and animation. Scarlet Studios has an artistic approach that aims to deliver products with unique and conceptually sound designs and aspects. Our founder has a fine arts background coupled with commercial experience that creates a hybrid look that satisfies both the artistic and marketable sides of photography. Scarlet Studios is based on Long Island and our photographers are equipped to travel any distance for a shoot.
